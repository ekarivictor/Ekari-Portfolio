I'm a designer with 3 years of experience. This experience spans across brand design, Product and motion Design.

I've been around design before I even called it that.

Started with drawing and thinking like an engineer, always curious about how things work, not just how they look. Games like Call of Duty and Apple's clean interfaces shaped how I see design: simple, immersive, and intentional.

I'm more of an innovator than a from-scratch creator. I take what exists and make it better, cleaner, smoother.

Problem-solving is what I enjoy most. If it doesn't feel effortless, it's not done.

Design, for me, is both craft and curiosity. I do it for the outcome and the understanding behind it.

And honestly, I keep doing it because I have to.

Now, I'm focused on turning that into something bigger, building products that actually live and work in the real world.

Philosophy: Make it exist Perfect it later
